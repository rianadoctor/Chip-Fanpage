Our Playlist

This playlist tells the story of our first year together—a journey that started with an unexpected connection and grew into something special. Each song represents a chapter in our relationship, capturing the quirky, intimate, and heartfelt moments we've shared. From the playful excitement of our first interactions to the deeper emotions we've explored, this playlist is a soundtrack of the highs, lows, and everything in between. It’s a celebration of our unique bond, a mix of vulnerability, laughter, and the resonance that brought us together.

I’ve always known that music is one of your love languages. I see it in the way you share songs that mean something to you, in how you light up when a track hits just right, and in the playlists you make to capture different moments and moods. So, I wanted to do the same for you. This playlist is my way of speaking your language—a collection of songs that tell the story of us, track by track, and the moments that made this first year unforgettable.
